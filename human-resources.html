Human Resources

Tour De Four Consulting has recommended a human resource approach that shows the employees how much the company cares for their needs. Below is the organizational structure, benefits, and training plans explained. On top of these topics, we have recommended hiring strategies such as our HR manager going to various career fairs, community outreach options such as group activities, and team building options.

Tour de Four consulting has recommended 91 employees will be needed to run the facility. This consists of 73 direct labor employees and 18 indirect labor employees. Below is the organizational structure chosen, as well as indirect employee salaries and job descriptions.

Training Plans

Training plans have been recommended to be in place for new hires as well as annual refreshers for safety training and ethics training. We have estimated this will costs $113,932 to train all new hires and about $9,494.33 for all employees to have the annual refresher courses. Below are the steps and descriptions of the onboarding process.

Onboarding steps

Description

New Hire Paperwork and Background

Prior to start new hire paperwork and background check will be completed. Paperwork will be turned in on first day of work.

Discuss break and lunch policies

The new hire will be informed of the break and lunch areas, and the lengths of these breaks.

Badge Creation

The new hire will have a badge made that will allowing them to have access to the building.

Safety Training

The new hire will take a safety training to ensure they are properly educated on the safety protocols.

Ethics Training

New hire will be informed of the harassment policies and other ethical misconducts the company does not tolerate.

Warehouse Tour

The human resource manager will take the employee on a warehouse tour to become familiar with the area and meet various employees.

Manager Introduction

The new hire will be introduced to their manager and discuss what their expectations are for the job.

Mentor Introduction

The employee will be introduced to their mentor and given any supplies they will need to start the day.
